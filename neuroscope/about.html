About the NeuroScope

This platform is designed as an engaging educational resource for high school students and adults eager to explore the realities of neurodiversity.

Our mission is to enhance awareness in social, academic, and professional spaces, fostering a greater appreciation for the diverse strengths and abilities within our communities.

Although NeuroScope is entirely student-led, our website draws upon insights from administrators, PhD candidates, speech pathologists, parents, and other experts deeply involved in the field of neurodiversity.
