Top 10 Things to Know About Neuroscience

Explore key insights about Neuroscience.

  • 1. Neuroscience explores the brain's structure and function.
  • 2. The human brain contains approximately 86 billion neurons.
  • 3. Neuroplasticity allows the brain to adapt and change.
  • 4. Neuroscience spans biology, psychology, and medicine.
  • 5. Brain imaging technologies include MRI and fMRI.
  • 6. The nervous system comprises the central and peripheral systems.
  • 7. Neurotransmitters are chemical messengers in the brain.
  • 8. Sleep plays a vital role in brain health and function.
  • 9. Neuroscience contributes to understanding mental health disorders.
  • 10. Ongoing research focuses on curing neurodegenerative diseases.
