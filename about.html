Welcome to the NeuroScope!

This website was created to be a helpful learning tool for high-school-age students and adults who wish to learn more about the reality of neurodiversity.

The goal of its distribution is to increase the level of awareness in social, academic and professional environments so we may better recognize all of our different strengths and aptitudes.
