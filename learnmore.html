      Synesthesia

      Another neurodivergent condition, where an individual's brain reroutes sensory information through multiple normally unrelated senses simultaneously. The result? People can experience things like how words taste, how music smells and what colors pop up when they hear their name called.

      Movie: Front of the Class

      2008 film based on the true story of Brad Cohen, a man with Tourette syndrome who overcomes significant challenges to achieve his dream of becoming a teacher. The film follows Brad from childhood, as he struggles with the stigma and misunderstanding surrounding his condition, through adulthood, where his perseverance and positivity inspire others. Despite being rejected by numerous schools during job interviews due to his tics, Brad finds a school that embraces his difference. The movie highlights the power of resilience, self-acceptance, and the impact of an empathetic teacher on students' lives.
